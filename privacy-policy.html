Privacy Policy

Life Countdown · EVC Engage · Effective March 17, 2026

EVC Engage ("we", "us", or "our") built Life Countdown (bundle ID: com.evcengage.lifecountdown) as a personal utility app. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information.

The short version: Life Countdown does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The app asks you to enter:

  • Your birth date
  • Your expected life expectancy

This data is stored exclusively on your device using AsyncStorage (local device storage). It is never transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

2. How Your Data Is Used

The birth date and life expectancy you provide are used solely to calculate and display countdowns within the app. This processing happens entirely on your device.

3. Local Notifications

Life Countdown may schedule local push notifications on your device to remind you of milestones. These notifications are created and triggered entirely on-device by the operating system. No notification service, server, or third party is involved.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not share any data with third parties. The app contains:

  • No analytics or tracking tools
  • No advertising frameworks
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data
  • No account creation or login system

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can delete your data by:

  • Clearing the app's data in your device settings
  • Uninstalling the app, which removes all associated local data

We do not retain any data on servers because we do not operate any servers that receive your data.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Life Countdown is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the app does not collect or transmit any data, no child data is at risk. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided information through the app, please contact us and we will assist you.

7. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA): since Life Countdown does not collect, process, or store any personal data on external servers, the typical data-processing obligations under the General Data Protection Regulation do not apply. All data remains under your sole control on your device. You may exercise your right to erasure at any time by deleting the app.

8. Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features, including device encryption and access controls such as passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID. Since no data leaves your device, there is no risk of server-side data breaches.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
